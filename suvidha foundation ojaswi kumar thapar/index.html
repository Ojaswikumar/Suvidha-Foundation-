ABOUT US

Suvidha Mahila Mandal is a non-profit dedicated to providing education to financially disadvantaged groups, empowering young minds to build a promising future. The organization offers student internships, mentorship, and volunteer opportunities. Through these programs, Suvidha Mahila Mandal aims to deliver innovative, lasting education that prepares students for an unpredictable future.
